Health Safety & Environment
Summit
Qatar-2017

The HSE Summit Qatar will take place from the 9th May 2017, INTERCONTINENTAL HOTEL Doha, Qatar. The summit will bring together senior executives from across the region for a series of workshops, case studies and high-level networking.

The event aims to solve key business challenges. In particular, the ability to network and learn from industry peers and service providers.

Working in partnership with our network of senior executives we identify the key industry themes. These form the foundation of our summit and permeate every layer of the content-rich safety and security program.

The health, safety and environment is a growing concern and affects all organisations. It is crucial to comply with regional polices and meet legal obligations, while providing employees with safe and healthy workplaces as the advancements in technology is creating new occupational hazards, making HSE an ever-growing concern worldwide.

Now, every organisation believes that their people are their most valuable asset and wants to foster a safe and healthy work environment not only for increasing productivity but also to promote healthy work culture as they understand the inter-dependability of operational excellence & health- safety practices. Additionally, the organisation is responsible to protect the environment and communities in which they do business.

This conference will bring these HSE experts together to share their knowledge and experience. The highlights will be on the latest technologies, methodologies, practices and key trends in reducing lost time, risk identification, HSE management systems, behavioural safety, fatigue management, fire safety, etc. Participants at the summit will include important government bodies, regulatory authorities, health, safety & environment professionals along with global solution providers to name a few. This will be a knowledge sharing platform and a gathering to bring in expertise from all across the Middle East & the globe.
