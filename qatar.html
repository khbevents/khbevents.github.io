Qatar Human Resources Summit
24th August 2017 Intercontinental Hotel, Doha ,Qatar

QATAR has been at the forefront of innovation and inspiration. Be it record-breaking projects or jaw-dropping spectacles, it has always captivated the world's hearts. Technology has revolutionized the way humans live and also has greatly impacted the way organizations function many times over. HR has moved beyond its role and transformed into a strategic business function of its own.

Successful businesses throughout the world are transforming the efficiency of their organizations through new HR innovations. Those which don't are being left behind. HR performance has become inextricably linked to the overall health and day-to-day running of businesses large and small; whether local or international. It is critical for every HR professional to be aware of these vast changes, while business leaders must implement recent innovations to stay competitive.

We are now in the brink of a revolution of Digital Human Resource in the world and especially in the Middle East Region as HR leaders are looking to latest technologies to make smart business decisions. From biggest government bodies to large private organizations and International Solution providers coming together to JOIN the Qatar HR summit, the priorities have changed. And we would cover all the latest trends from Cloud, Social, Mobile, Employee Engagement, HR Analytics, gamification as well as traditional HR Technology processes.
